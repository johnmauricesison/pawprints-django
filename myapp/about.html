ABOUT USPawPrints: Where Every Paw MattersWelcome to our platform dedicated to the welfare of animals in the Philippines. We are passionate about making a difference in the lives of stray and abandoned animals, promoting responsible pet ownership, and advocating for their rights.Our MissionAt our core, we are driven by a mission to create a more compassionate society where every animal is treated with kindness, respect, and dignity. Through our platform, we strive to:

Rescue and rehabilitate stray and abandoned animals, providing them with the care and love they deserve.

Educate the community about the importance of responsible pet ownership, including proper care, training, and health considerations.

Raise awareness about animal welfare issues and advocate for stronger protections and policies to prevent cruelty and neglect.

Facilitate adoptions to find loving forever homes for homeless animals and reduce the stray population.What We DoRescue and Rehabilitation
